Project Description

The project is a short interactive 360 movie that places the spectator in the body of a couple of tourists who visit a place called The Pyramids of Vidy located in the city of Lausanne in Switzerland.
During the visit, something supernatural is happening that drives the tourists running away the place.

The idea is to create two 360 films to allow the spectator switching its point of view to either tourist #1 or #2.
To make those two films, I have 2 options :

  1. Placing a 360 camera on each actor. But I don't have two cameras and even if I had two cameras, one will be visible by the other.
  2. Using one camera and make the film twice. This option requires to have same synchronization points. A clap is necessary on each scene and the actors placement must be very precise.
The option #2 seems more complicated but I like the challenge and I don't want to see the second camera. So, let's go for the option #2.

The movie will bring emotions like joy visiting a nice place, surpise, stress and fear.

Pre-Production

Locating the place

I used Google Map screenshots to have a good idea of the distances and to imagine the actors path and waypoints.

Next I did few normal videos of the real location. This helped me to see what would be the landscape and how long could last the movie.
